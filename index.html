Seth Manilove: The Not-So-Typical Pre-Medical Student


Scientist, Speaker, Philanthropist

Seth Manilove is a recent graduate of the University of Miami. Seth used college to pursue his wide variety of interests as he earned a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Biology as well as a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Economics. Seth believes that in order to be a well-rounded individual one must pursue more than just one interest.

Seth is passionate about giving back to the Jewish community, the advancement of science research, and hopefully practicing sports medicine.

Seth Manilove was a Panelist at the Greater Miami Jewish Federation's Women's Philanthropy Event: How to Keep Judaism Alive - Carrying on Jewish Tradition
